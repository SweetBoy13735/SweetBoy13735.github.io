About me

Learn more about who I am and what I do!

Greetings all!

Hi, I'm Ramone Graham (Or "Sweetboy13735" if you're using my online alias). I'm an 18 year-old student interested in the field of Game Design and Development.

Aspiring programmer,

Since the age of 10, I've always been astounded at new technologies and the code used to build them. With my curiousity peaked, I started learning how to program using basic applications such as Scratch, and even logic controllers such as Millenium 3. Now I've taken these concepts and set my sights on all sorts of coding languages, notable Java, HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Founder of HyperByte Industries,

Being able to build my own computer games is something that I hold close to my heart. With that in mind, a few friends and I founded HyperByte Industries; an electronic entertainment company with the purpose of creating games that others can enjoy. Our debut title, "Pixel Paintballers", is a simple, open-source action-shooter where the only objective is to shoot down every mob that isn't your friend.

And a proud gamer.

Not only do I enjoy creating games, but I enjoy playing them and sharing those experiences with others on my YouTube and Twitch channels. I've also used this hobby to help empower the wider community, taking part in charity live-streams such as "CHILL-19" in collaboration with "The Pseudo Dopamine Centre".
