I do a li'l bit of bar-tending, baristing, coding, graphic design, production, streaming, and just about anything else that my "learn-while-doing" approach to life wills for me.

But that's honestly a mouthful to say, so we'll stick with my slogan as my main three descriptions of myself. Thanks, whimsical brain, and you, for stopping by!

Since the age of 10, I started programming using applications such as Scratch, and logic controllers such as Millenium 3. I then applied these concepts into coding languages and ecosystems, most notably Java and Web development (HTML, CSS and JS).

In addition to software development, I've developed an interest for broadcast production set-up and operation, which have proven useful for helping power Asura's Rogue Summit Series.

Night-rider.

"Abstraction, Emulation, Comparison.
A continuous cycle of processing one's own mind until a sense of self-understanding is achieved, or the Icarus journey reaches its end." - NGHT-RDR

Before I explain, let's be clear. Night-riding isn't "inciting violence" for thrills; nor is it "seeking revenge" for wrong-doings against me.
Night-riding is a state of mind; an interface to visualise the World in a more focussed manner; a process of self-evaluation and improvement to be more than what limits define.

All of these feelings are woven into every element within my brand, carefully calculated and meticulously executed. The "Glitched S" type-face, an accent disintegrating the tail-end, and a translation of the World into Night-rider's vision, applied onto full-body works.

Gamer.

Some consider games to be a waste of time, an excuse to be lazy, or even a prominent reason for youth violence. Others may see it as a form of entertainment, an exciting career opportunity, or to raise awareness in society.
To me, games aren't just a hobby; they're a culmination of compulsive planning, hours of programming, captivating music, and possibly much more; a unique blend of technical precision, human talent and natural beauty that, if well executed, has the potential to powerfully immerse a consumer in a completely different experience.

I've always wanted to be a part of a group which uses games as a way to not only showcase themselves but benefit the wider World around them. I'm proud to be the Chief Technical Officer of Pseudo Dopamine; A friendly community of content creatives, technology enthusiasts, and above all, gamers.
