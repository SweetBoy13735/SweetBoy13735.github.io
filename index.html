Hi, I'm Ramone Graham.
I enjoy moulding different physical and digital systems together to enhance experiences and create new opportunities.

I do a li'l bit of bar-tending, baristing, coding, graphic design, production, streaming, and just about anything else that my "learn-while-doing" approach to life wills for me.

But that's honestly a mouthful to say, so we'll stick with my slogan as my main three descriptions of myself. Thanks, whimsical brain, and you, for stopping by!

Since the age of 10, I started programming using applications and controllers, notably Scratch and Millenium 3. I then applied these concepts into coding languages and ecosystems, most notably Java and Web development (HTML, CSS and JS).

Following a few charity fundraising events in association with Pseudo Dopamine and Asura, I've also delved into livestream production, usually operating OBS Studio and implementing additional infrastructure if required.

Night-rider.

Abstraction. Emulation. Comparison.
A continuous cyle of processing one's own mind until a sense of self-understanding is achieved, or the Icarus journey reaches its end.

Before I explain, let's be clear. Night-riding isn't "inciting violence" for thrills; nor is it "seeking revenge" for wrong-doings against me.
It's a state of mind; an interface to visualise the World in a focussed manner; a process of evaluation and improvement to be more than what limits define.

All of these ideals are woven into every element within my brand, meticulously calculated and carefully executed. The "Glitched S" type-face, an accent disintegrating the tail-end, and a translation of the World into Night-rider's vision, applied onto full-body works.

Gamer.

Games aren't just a hobby; they're a culmination of compulsive planning, hours of programming, captivating music, and possibly much more; a unique blend of technical precision, human talent and natural beauty that, if well executed, has the potential to immerse someone in a completely different experience.

I've always wanted to be a part of a group which uses games as a way to not only showcase themselves but benefit the wider World around them. I'm proud to be the Chief Technical Officer of Pseudo Dopamine; A friendly community of content creatives, technology enthusiasts, and above all, gamers.
